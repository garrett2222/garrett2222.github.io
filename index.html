Short Report Scraper

As I mentioned, I worked at a proprietary trading firm. There was a good opportunity to make money when a reputable source released a new short report. However, the trade was only really great it you could get the ticker of the report as soon as the report was published. If you figured out the ticker 2 seconds late you would be hitting bottom prints and get your face ripped off as market makers stepped in (until they realized what was going on).

I was dissatisfied with speed of the 3rd party scraper most traders used at the firm so I set out to build a better one. While most websites dislike, or eventually block you, for scrapping their websites at sub-second intervals I knew that it was possible. Using object oriented python I built a script that put each website on its on thread and then split the threads across all four cores — again, fractions of seconds were extremely important.
